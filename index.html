BRUSSEL SPROUT CHEDDAR CHEESE BAKE

Learn More

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 slices thick-cut apple smoked bacon, diced
  • 1 1/2 cup onion, small diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 pound Brussels sprouts, thinly sliced lengthwise (about 1/8-inch)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/4 cup chicken stock or water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cup shredded sharp cheddar

Steps

Step One

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit convectional bake. Also shred the cheese.

Step Two

Chop the thyme, mince the garlic and chop the onion and set them aside, rinse the brussle sprouts, slice the brussle sprouts length wise, and split them into two piles.

Watch

Step Three

Dice the bacon, and sauté the bacon until golden brown (approximatly 8 minutes). With a slotted spoon remove the bacon and place it in a heat proof bowl, and set it aside.

Watch

Step Four

Add onions to the pan with bacon grease and cook for 4 minutes or until wilted and beginning to caramelize.




Watch

Step Five

Add in garlic and cook for an addition 2 minutes. Using a slotted spoon transfer the onion and garlic into the bowl with the bacon.

Step Six

Add 1 tablespoons of butter to the pan along with half the Brussels sprouts and 1/2 teaspoon thyme. Cook the sprouts for 2 to 3 minutes on one side or until they begin to brown. Add 1/2 the chicken stock, 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper, 1/8 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspoon pepper. Flip the Brussels sprouts and continue to cook for another minute. Remove them from the pan to the bowl with the bacon and onions.

Step Seven

Add the remaining 1 tablespoons of butter and cook the remaining Brussels sprouts in the same way, adding the remaining 1/8 cup of stock after they’ve browned, and seasoning them with the remaining 1/8 teaspoon salt and 1/8 teaspoon pepper.

Step Eight

Mix all the Brussels sprouts that were made in with the bacon, onion, and garlic in a casserole dish.

Step Nine

Put the cheese on the top of the casserole dish and bake it for 20 minutes
